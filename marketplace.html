Play-to-earn

How it works

01

Play a game

Hit the score threshold set in the game manifest to trigger a reward claim.

02

Claim OKT

okama-run signs and submits the claim to the OkamaLabs relay, which mints OKT to your wallet.

03

Buy NFT assets

Spend OKT in the marketplace for game-specific OKAssets that enhance your gameplay.

04

Assets unlock content

OkamaOS injects your NFT inventory via OKAMA_ASSETS_PATH so games know what you own.
